Online Distribution & Fulfillment Services

Placing orders on your personalized online storefront with ImageX is as easy as clicking a mouse!  Your storefront will provide access to your specific catalog, populated with the products you already buy and print. You define your own ordering environment—categories and subcategories, buying groups and cost centers to match your company's business processes.

With the added benefit of working with ImageX’s Teams, your storefront can also be used to order print-on-demand materials such as business cards, letterhead, forms and brochures, custom apparel, promotional giveaways for events, and even gifts and awards all personalized with your logo.

  • Online ordering & fulfillment saves time and streamlines the ordering process for your employees
  • Allows you to save money by ordering your supplies in bulk to get the best pricing
  • Our secure Bay Area and Midwest distribution & fulfillment centers eliminate the need for extra storage space at your facility
  • Ordering capabilities are permission and password protected, ensuring security, order & inventory reporting, and activity tracking capabilities for each user
  • Integrated messaging capabilities will keep you informed as orders are placed and as your order status changes
  • Inventory management at your fingertips: timely notices are sent when an item needs to be re-ordered and you will have access to inventory & order history reports at your convenience
Contact us to get started with your own personalized storefront.
