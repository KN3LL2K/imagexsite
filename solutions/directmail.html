Direct Mail

Direct mail has long proven to be an effective way to reach current and potential customers, even in the digital age. According to a 2016 US Postal Service Survey, overall mail engagement has increased since 2012, and Millennials are as at least engaged with the mail as non-Millennials*. ImageX has the experience and expertise to guide you through the maze of strict postal requirements and the approval process. We can provide you with targeted mailing lists to help you reach the group of recipients that exactly matches your criteria. We can also suggest cost-effective mailing options based on your needs. Capabilities include:

  • Full Database Services
  • Targeted List Acquisition with NCOA and CASS Certification
  • Self and Two-way Mailers
  • Variable Data Printing and Ink Jetting
  • Fulfillment and Kitting
Contact us to discuss how we can help you reach your customer base with direct mailing services!

*Source: USPS Mail Moments 2016 Review, Conducted by Summit Research
